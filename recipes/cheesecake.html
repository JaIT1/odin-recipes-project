Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup HONEY MAID graham crackers
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 4(8oz) packages of cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 325
  • Mix crumbs, sugar and butter; press firmly onto bottom of pan
  • Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with mixer
  • Add eggs 1 at a time
  • Pour over crust
  • Bake 55 min
