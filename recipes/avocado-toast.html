Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Toast bread until golden and firm
  2. Remove pit from avocado
  3. Scoop the insides into a bowl
    • Mash until smooth
  4. Mix in a pinch of salt
  5. Enjoy!
